Frequently Asked Questions

Caddie Club is Canada’s first golf apparel membership that combines AI styling, digital avatars, and premium Canadian golf brands — all powered by a unique membership model, AI fit technology and styling engine. Here’s how it works. 

  • Take the Quiz: Complete our 60-second style quiz to generate your fit and style profile.
  • Match with Your Avatar: Based on your profile, you'll be matched to a Caddie Crew avatar (Elite, Bold, or Chill) who represents your style. Caddie AI will use this to recommend full outfits, one-off pieces, and limited-time apparel drops tailored just for you. 
  • Visualize your Style: See exactly how your outfit will look — down to stitching and buttons — using an immersive 3D avatar engine powered by Caddie AI. As you browse, your avatar updates in real-time so you can preview how each piece fits your look. 
  • Become a Member: Select a membership tier that fits your budget and shopping habits. Every dollar you pay becomes Caddie Credits — store credit you can use anytime. No monthly pressure. No wasted spend. Just stack your credits and shop when you're ready. 
  • Join the Community: Your avatar is your personal golf style identity. You can customize it anytime. In the future, you’ll be able to personalize gear — from apparel to golf balls — and represent your avatar in tournaments, leagues, and more.
