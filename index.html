Frequently Asked Questions

Caddie Club is Canada’s first golf apparel membership that combines AI styling, digital avatars, and premium Canadian brands — all powered by a unique credit-based system and AI fit and styling engine. Here’s how it works. 

  • Take the Quiz: Complete our quick 60-second style quiz.
  • Match with Your Avatar / Brand: Based on your fit, style, and on-course vibe, choose which caddie crew member best represents you (Elite, Bold, Chill). 
  • Personalized Experience: Our AI system (Caddie) will recommend gear from premium Canadian brands perfectly suited to the Avatar you chose. 
  • Visualize your Style: See exactly how each piece looks on your avatar through our immersive 3D model feature built by Caddie AI. 
  • Buy with Confidence:  As you consider purchasing styles recommended to you, your avatar will change to your selections, and you will see the apparel on a 3D model to show exactly what it looks like on a human. 
  • Purchase & Subscribe: Purchase your style, and opt in to subscribe to a monthly membership that matches your budget and now your style and fit profile
    • Your monthly membership turns into Caddie Credits (store credit).
    • Use your credits to shop curated drop boxes, full outfits, or one-off pieces whenever you’re ready.
  • Join the Community: Customize your look and avatars at anytime. And in the future, you will be able to order custom gear, balls, etc all with your own personal avatar and brand.
